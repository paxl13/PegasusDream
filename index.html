Boots of Fury

"Boots of Fury" is an action-packed roguelike game developed for the Pico-8 platform. Inspired by classics like The Legend of Zelda and Hades, this game puts you in the boots of a small knight with a big problem: There is a lot of baddie that wants his skin!

Game Description

Embark on a fury-filled quest to take down your former friends in a series of challenging encounters. Explore diverse environments, discover hidden secrets, and upgrade your abilities to become the ultimate buddy-busting knight.

Features

  • Fast-paced, action-packed combat
  • Roguelike elements with permadeath and procedural generation
  • Charming 8-bit graphics and sound
  • Multiple weapons and upgrades
  • Challenging boss battles
  • Secrets to discover
